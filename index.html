Dead Wait Times

Patrons wait 30–60 minutes with nothing to do. They get restless, stop ordering, and eventually leave.

Revenue Plateau

Traditional karaoke doesn't incentivize spending. No connection between queue position and bar tab.

Low Engagement

Only 1 person sings at a time. Everyone else is just sitting there — bored.

Missed F&B Sales

No easy way to order from the table. Staff can't keep up during peak hours. Orders slip through the cracks.

How It Works

Three things.
That's it.

1
📱 Scan & Join

Patron scans a QR code at their table. Sets a stage name, picks a song and an instrument. Their band joins the queue automatically.

No app download. No account creation. Takes about 30 seconds.
2
🎸 Play Together

The band takes the stage — Vocals, guitar, and drums. Keys can even play from the table for the stage-shy. Dynamic difficulty makes everyone sound great.

4 people on stage instead of 1. More energy, more engagement, more fun.
3
🛒 Order From Your Seat

While waiting (or anytime), patrons browse your full food and drink menu on their phone and order directly. Orders go to your bar or kitchen tablet instantly.

Higher tabs. Faster service. Spending more means playing sooner.
